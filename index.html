Assessment Framework

Explain → Evaluate → Execute

Every course uses a uniform 3-stage assessment principle ensuring knowledge, judgment, and practical capability are all tested.

📚

Explain

Knowledge checks using multiple-choice questions to verify conceptual understanding and correct mental models.

⚖️

Evaluate

Scenario-based judgment tests that place learners in realistic situations requiring sound decision-making.

🛠️

Execute

Practical tasks or artifacts demonstrating real-world capability — the evidence base for NCVET certification.

🎓

Certified & Credited

Independent assessors, standard rubrics, and NCrF credit allocation upon successful course completion.

Design Principles

Built on Five Core Principles

Every course, every assessment, every delivery decision is anchored to these five commitments.

📋
Principle 01

NCVET-Ready by Design

Every course has defined learning outcomes, assessment criteria, and a summative assessment — not retrofitted.

🌐
Principle 02

Online-First Delivery

Scalable, PAN-India accessible. Hybrid only for select longer programs where facilitation adds value.

🧰
Principle 03

Tool-Agnostic Curriculum

Future-proof learning that doesn't lock learners to a single vendor or platform — skills transfer across tools.

Principle 04

Safety as Baseline

Responsible AI, cyber hygiene, and digital ethics are embedded in every course regardless of subject domain.

📂
Principle 05

Stackable Credits

NCrF-aligned credits accumulate across courses, enabling flexible, portable qualifications over a learner's lifetime.
